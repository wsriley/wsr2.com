Learn a little more about Warren Steven Riley Jr

October 30, 2011August 7, 2019 wsriley2

I am a software developer living in Springfield, VA who is focused on 3D, simulations and graphics applications.  Some of my personal passions include my cat Mr. Shiney, an interest (by no means expertise) in bio-gerontology and neuropharmacology as well as quantum mechanics.  I’m definitely an optimist who feels the world we want can be achieved but it’s up to us to create it.  This site will further flesh out any questions or curiosities you might have about the man we like to call Warren Steven Riley Jr.
